My SVG!

Above is my SVG. This is a picture of a drawing someone made on a sheet of paper. This drawing is of a tree on rolling hills, and the sheet of paper is pinned to something.

  • rect: The paper that the picture is drawn on.
  • circle: The pin that holds the paper to something else (a bulletin board, maybe?).
  • path: The hills.
  • polygon: The tree.
